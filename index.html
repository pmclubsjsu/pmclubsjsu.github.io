For the curious

So… what is product management?

A Product Manager (PM) is the person who answers the question: "What should we build, and why?"

A PM sits at the intersection of technology, business, and design. They understand users deeply, set strategy, and work with engineers and designers to ship products that people actually love.

PMs are some of the most sought-after roles in tech. Companies like Google, Apple, Meta, and thousands of startups in Silicon Valley are always looking for great PMs, and SJSU sits right in the middle of all of it.

You don't need a specific major to become a PM. The field rewards curiosity, communication, and the ability to think about problems from multiple angles — skills you're building right now, no matter what you study.

What PMs actually do every day
🎯
Define the vision Set goals for what the product should accomplish and why it matters to users
🔍
Talk to users Run interviews and surveys to understand real pain points and desires
🤝
Work cross-functionally Partner with engineers, designers, marketing, and leadership every day
📊
Make data-informed decisions Use metrics and research to prioritize what gets built next
🚀
Ship and iterate Launch features, measure impact, and keep improving based on feedback
